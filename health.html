Extreme use of testosterone: Is it safe or not?

Big muscles and perfect shape in a short time. The question is: are they really natural? Well, this trend is very popular among adults, but mostly among teenagers who seek stronger muscles by injecting testosterone. However, it's very dangerous because if you use too much, it can be fatal. A strong argument against this process is that people have already died because of the extreme use of testosterone.

One example is the influencer Jo Lindner, known as "Joesthetics," who died of an aneurysm—a condition in which, with testosterone use, the vein fills with cells and the pressure increases, causing the vein to tear. His death was confirmed by Nicha, his girlfriend. Testosterone use can have many effects, such as increasing muscle mass, reducing body fat, building strength, and increasing sexual desire. However, it's very dangerous. Think about a very young person dying because of dissatisfaction with their body. It's unnecessary; if you prefer a healthy body, you can simply go to the gym. It's important to learn about this because there is a high chance you could be harming yourself, so it's always good to do your research.

By: Maria Eduarda Figueroa

Is the extra use of testosterone good for your body?

This article will talk about esthetic use of testosterone. Nowadays, a lot of people use testosterone to look prettiest, healthy and strong. Using extra testosterone might not be recommendable because it can be dangerous for your body. People can have heart problems, kidney problems, acne, and much more.

People use testosterone to look healthier, stronger and prettiest. But it doesn't make sense to harm your health to look more beautiful !!! One example is the digital influencer, Jo Lindner. He died because of the excess of testosterone that made his vein to tear in his neck. This system is called Aneurysm. People put their life at risk because of their appearance.

One more reason is, you can have all these characteristics without testosterone. For example, eating healthy food, and going to the gym. One example of this is the nutritionist Rodrigo Góes. He uses his knowledge to change people's minds, that it is possible to have a healthy life without extra testosterone. And the same for being stronger.

It's recommendable to use other ways to get stronger, healthy and prettier instead of extra testosterone because of your health. It is so dangerous to use testosterone without scientific proof, and maybe change their minds about it.

By: Alice Reis

Using testosterone worth it?

If you are using extra testosterone I have some news, you will have some problems in the future. And I am not saying that just to scare you, because a lot of people had some problems, even dying. Jo Lindner is an example, that injected it to gain muscles. Extra testosterone is a medication that makes you stronger. People inject it to get more muscles in less time, however, will also come heart and kidney problems, breast development, baldness, acne and testicle atrophy, which shrinks the testes. Doing it is a waste of time, since it's very unhealthy. In spite of that, you should go to the gym, in view of the fact that it's the healthiest attitude.

Although, in some cases extra testosterone is necessary, such as when the person may not produce sufficient testosterone, even so in most of the cases bodybuilders inject more than the demand, still it is not obligatory, for the reason that they already train hard, why do people inject this hormone?! Even the president of the Brazilian Society of Urology says that it is only for those who need it.

Just to remind, extra hormones are unproductive as it is able to cause many health problems, and even death. Training at the gym with the right healthcare is the least path for a better life.
